Frequently Asked Questions

When will Digital Companion™ launch?

Digital Companion™ is currently in development and will be launching soon. Join our beta program to be among the first to access our service and receive special pricing.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial so you can experience Digital Companion™ before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are securely processed.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Are there any discounts available?

Beta testers will receive special pricing. We're also developing programs for those with financial need. Contact us for more information.
