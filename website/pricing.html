Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the free trial work?

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You'll only be charged when you decide to continue after the trial period ends.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption to protect your data. Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What if I need help choosing a plan?

Our support team is available to help you choose the right plan for your needs. Contact us at annie@digitalcompanion.co.uk or use the chat feature on our website.

Do you offer discounts for annual subscriptions?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount when you choose annual billing. This option will be available after your free trial period.
