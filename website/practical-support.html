Practical Support When You Need It Most

We simplify the administrative burden that comes with loss. From managing accounts and documents to navigating legal processes, our tools make complex tasks manageable.

Administrative Dashboard

Our intuitive dashboard brings organization to a chaotic time, giving you a clear overview of all tasks, documents, and progress in one place.

The dashboard features:

  • Personalized task checklist with progress tracking
  • Priority indicators for time-sensitive tasks
  • Document organization and storage
  • Calendar with important dates and reminders
  • Contact management for important communications

Everything is designed to be simple and accessible, even during times of emotional stress. You can access your dashboard from any device, ensuring you have guidance wherever you are.

Organized & Accessible

"The dashboard made an overwhelming list of tasks feel manageable. I could see exactly what needed to be done, what was in progress, and what I'd already completed. It gave me a sense of control during a time when everything felt chaotic."

— Rebecca M., using Digital Companion since 2024

Secure Document Vault

Keep all important documents organized, secure, and accessible when you need them most. Our document vault provides bank-level security with the simplicity of a digital filing cabinet.

The document vault includes:

  • Secure storage for death certificates, wills, and other important documents
  • Document categorization and tagging for easy retrieval
  • Ability to share specific documents with family members or professionals
  • Document templates for common letters and forms
  • Automatic reminders for document submission deadlines

All documents are encrypted and protected, giving you peace of mind that sensitive information remains secure while still being accessible when needed.

Everything in One Place

"I needed to access the death certificate multiple times for different organizations. Having it securely stored in the vault meant I could retrieve it instantly without having to search through physical papers each time. The document templates also saved me hours of writing formal letters."

— Thomas K., using Digital Companion since 2024

Account Management

Simplify the process of notifying organizations and managing accounts after a loss. Our account management tools help you track, notify, and close accounts efficiently.

Account management features include:

  • Comprehensive checklist of organizations to notify
  • Tracking system for account notifications and closures
  • Template notification letters for different types of accounts
  • Guidance on required documentation for each organization
  • Secure record-keeping of all communications

We provide specific guidance for different types of accounts, from utilities and banking to social media and subscription services, ensuring nothing is overlooked.

Simplified Notifications

"I had no idea how many organizations needed to be notified after my father passed away. The account management system gave me a complete list and helped me track which ones I'd contacted and which still needed attention. It was one less thing to worry about."

— Jennifer P., using Digital Companion since 2024

Legal Guidance

Navigate complex legal processes with clear, step-by-step guidance tailored to your specific situation and location. Our legal guidance simplifies probate, estate administration, and other legal requirements.

Legal guidance includes:

  • Location-specific information about probate and estate administration
  • Explanations of legal terms and processes in plain language
  • Checklists for required legal documents and deadlines
  • Guidance on when to seek professional legal advice
  • Resources for finding qualified legal professionals

While we don't provide legal advice, we offer clear explanations and structured guidance to help you understand and navigate legal requirements during this challenging time.

Clear Direction

"The legal guidance demystified the probate process for me. Instead of legal jargon, I got clear explanations and a step-by-step approach. It helped me understand what I could handle myself and when I needed to consult with a solicitor."

— David W., using Digital Companion since 2024

Financial Tools

Manage financial matters with confidence using our specialized tools designed to help you track expenses, identify benefits, and organize financial information.

Financial tools include:

  • Expense tracker for funeral and estate administration costs
  • Benefits eligibility checker for bereavement support
  • Asset and liability inventory system
  • Financial document organizer
  • Guidance on handling joint accounts and transferring assets

Our financial tools help bring clarity to financial matters during a time when managing money may be the last thing on your mind, but still requires attention.

Financial Clarity

"The expense tracker was invaluable for keeping all the funeral and estate costs organized. I also wouldn't have known about the bereavement benefits I was eligible for if not for the benefits checker. It made a real difference during a financially stressful time."

— Michelle S., using Digital Companion since 2024
