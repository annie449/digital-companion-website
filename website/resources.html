Reading Materials

Our team has curated a selection of books that many find helpful during the grief journey. These recommendations span different grief experiences and approaches to healing.

It's OK That You're Not OK

By Megan Devine

A refreshingly honest approach to grief that challenges conventional wisdom and offers practical guidance for living with loss.

Learn More

The Year of Magical Thinking

By Joan Didion

A powerful memoir about the year following the death of the author's husband, exploring grief, memory, and the mind's response to loss.

Learn More

Option B

By Sheryl Sandberg & Adam Grant

Combining personal insights with research on finding strength in the face of adversity, this book offers practical strategies for building resilience.

Learn More

Bearing the Unbearable

By Joanne Cacciatore

A compassionate guide to navigating grief and loss, written by a bereaved mother and grief counselor who understands profound loss firsthand.

Learn More
