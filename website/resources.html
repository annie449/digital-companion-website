Recommended Reading

It's OK That You're Not OK

Megan Devine

A refreshingly honest approach to grief that rejects the idea that grief is something to "get over" and instead offers a path to living alongside loss.

View Details

The Year of Magical Thinking

Joan Didion

A powerful memoir about the year following the death of the author's husband, exploring the irrational nature of grief and the human capacity for resilience.

View Details

Option B

Sheryl Sandberg & Adam Grant

Combining personal insights with research on finding strength in the face of adversity, this book offers practical strategies for building resilience.

View Details

Bearing the Unbearable

Joanne Cacciatore

A compassionate guide to navigating grief and loss, written by a bereaved mother and grief counselor who understands the terrain of loss firsthand.

View Details

The Wild Edge of Sorrow

Francis Weller

Explores the sacred work of grief, offering a new vision of grief and sorrow as a pathway to a deeper connection with ourselves and the world.

View Details

When Things Fall Apart

Pema Chödrön

Drawing from Buddhist wisdom, this book offers guidance on how to use painful emotions to cultivate wisdom, compassion, and courage.

Support Organizations

Cruse Bereavement Care

The UK's largest bereavement charity, providing free care and support to people experiencing grief.

  • www.cruse.org.uk
  • helpline@cruse.org.uk
  • Helpline: 0808 808 1677

Services: One-to-one support, local support groups, telephone support, resources for children and young people.

GriefShare

A network of grief support groups meeting worldwide, with a structured program to help people move from mourning to joy.

  • www.griefshare.org
  • info@griefshare.org

Services: Support groups, online community, daily email encouragement, video seminars.

What's Your Grief

An online resource promoting grief education, exploration, and expression in both practical and creative ways.

  • whatsyourgrief.com
  • whatsyourgrief@gmail.com

Services: Articles, podcasts, online courses, webinars, resources for professionals.

The Compassionate Friends

A peer support organization offering friendship and understanding to bereaved parents, siblings, and grandparents.

  • www.tcf.org.uk (UK)
  • www.compassionatefriends.org (US)
  • UK Helpline: 0345 123 2304

Services: Local support groups, online forums, retreats, memorial events, resources for different types of loss.

Mind

A mental health charity providing advice and support to anyone experiencing mental health problems, including grief-related issues.

  • www.mind.org.uk
  • info@mind.org.uk
  • Infoline: 0300 123 3393

Services: Information resources, local support services, online community, advocacy.

Samaritans

A charity providing emotional support to anyone in emotional distress or struggling to cope, including those experiencing grief.

  • www.samaritans.org
  • jo@samaritans.org
  • Helpline: 116 123 (UK)

Services: 24/7 helpline, email support, self-help app, outreach programs.
