Initial Assessment

Our compassionate approach begins with understanding your unique situation. The initial assessment is designed to be simple yet thorough, gathering the information we need to provide meaningful support without overwhelming you during a difficult time.

Through a brief series of questions, we'll learn about:

Your relationship to the person you've lost

The timeframe of your loss

Immediate administrative concerns

Your support network

Your preferences for receiving emotional support

This assessment typically takes less than 10 minutes to complete, and you can save your progress and return to it at any time.

What to Expect

Personalized Plan

Within 24 hours of completing your assessment, you'll receive your personalized Care Plan. This comprehensive roadmap is designed to guide you through both the practical and emotional aspects of your grief journey.

Your Care Plan includes:

A prioritized checklist of administrative tasks

Recommended timeline for completing each task

Curated resources specific to your situation

Suggested support groups and community connections

Personalized emotional support recommendations

Your plan is dynamic and will evolve as your needs change. You can update your preferences at any time, and our system will adjust your plan accordingly.

Your Roadmap

Guided Support

With your personalized plan in place, we provide step-by-step guidance through each task and connection. Our platform combines intuitive tools with compassionate support to help you navigate both practical matters and emotional challenges.

Guided support features include:

Interactive task management with detailed instructions

Secure document storage and organization

Connection to community support groups

Access to professional resources when needed

Emotional support tools and resources

Our guidance is designed to be clear and actionable, breaking down complex processes into manageable steps.

Ongoing Care

Grief isn't linear, and your needs will change over time. Our ongoing care adapts to your journey, providing continued support as you navigate different phases of grief and loss.

Ongoing care includes:

Progress tracking to celebrate milestones

Plan adjustments based on changing needs

Regular check-ins at meaningful intervals

Long-term resources for continued healing

Community connection for lasting support

We're with you for as long as you need us, providing support that evolves with your journey.
