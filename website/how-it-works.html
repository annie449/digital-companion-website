Initial Assessment

Our compassionate approach begins with understanding your unique situation. The initial assessment is designed to be simple yet thorough, gathering the information we need to provide meaningful support without overwhelming you during a difficult time.

Through a brief series of questions, we'll learn about:

  • Your relationship to the person you've lost
  • The timeframe of your loss
  • Immediate administrative concerns
  • Your support network
  • Your preferences for receiving emotional support

This assessment typically takes less than 10 minutes to complete, and you can save your progress and return to it at any time.

Personalized Plan

Within 24 hours of completing your assessment, you'll receive your personalized Care Plan. This comprehensive roadmap is designed to guide you through both the practical and emotional aspects of your grief journey.

Your Care Plan includes:

  • A prioritized checklist of administrative tasks
  • Recommended timeline for completing each task
  • Curated resources specific to your situation
  • Suggested support groups and community connections
  • Personalized emotional support recommendations

Your plan is dynamic and will evolve as your needs change. You can update your preferences at any time, and our system will adjust your plan accordingly.

Guided Support

With your personalized plan in place, we provide step-by-step guidance through each task and connection. Our platform combines intuitive tools with compassionate support to help you navigate both practical matters and emotional challenges.

Guided support features include:

Our guidance is designed to empower you while providing the structure and support you need during this challenging time.

Ongoing Care

Grief isn't linear, and your needs will change over time. Our ongoing care adapts to your journey, providing continuous support that evolves as you move through different phases of grief and administrative tasks.

Ongoing care includes:

We're with you for as long as you need us, providing support that acknowledges both the immediate aftermath of loss and the long-term journey of grief.

Common Questions

How soon after a loss should I start using Digital Companion?

Digital Companion can be helpful at any stage of your grief journey. Some people find it valuable immediately after a loss when administrative tasks are most pressing, while others come to us months later when they're ready to address unresolved matters or seek emotional support. There's no "right time" - we meet you where you are.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

We've designed Digital Companion to be intuitive and accessible for users of all technical abilities. Our interface is simple and straightforward, with clear instructions at every step. If you ever need help, our support team is available to guide you through any challenges you might encounter.

Can I use Digital Companion if I'm supporting someone else who is grieving?

Yes, Digital Companion can be a valuable resource for those supporting others through grief. Our platform includes specific guidance for supporters, helping you understand how to best assist your loved one while also caring for yourself during this challenging time.

How is Digital Companion different from other grief support resources?

Digital Companion uniquely combines practical task management with emotional support in one integrated platform. While many resources focus on either the administrative aspects of loss or the emotional journey of grief, we recognize that these elements are interconnected and provide comprehensive support for both.
