Frequently Asked Questions

We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. Our team will get back to you as soon as they can.

If you need immediate emotional support, please contact a crisis support service such as:

  • Samaritans (UK): 116 123
  • Crisis Text Line (UK): Text SHOUT to 85258
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US): 1-800-273-8255

These services are available 24/7 and can provide immediate support. Digital Companion's support team is not equipped to handle crisis situations, but we can connect you with resources for ongoing support once the immediate crisis has passed.
