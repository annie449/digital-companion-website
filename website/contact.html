Frequently Asked Questions

When will Digital Companion™ be fully launched?

Digital Companion™ is currently in the final stages of development and will be launching soon. You can sign up for our notification list to be among the first to know when we're live.

How can I provide feedback on the platform?

We welcome your feedback! You can share your thoughts through our survey or by emailing us at feedback@digitalcompanion.com.

Is Digital Companion™ available internationally?

Initially, Digital Companion™ will be available in the United States, with plans to expand internationally in the future. Our resources section will be available worldwide.

How can organizations partner with Digital Companion™?

We're open to partnerships with grief support organizations, mental health professionals, and administrative service providers. Please contact us at partnerships@digitalcompanion.com to discuss collaboration opportunities.
